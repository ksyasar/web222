Explain the concept of Responsive Design. What does the term mean? How does responsive design relate to web technologies?


Responsive Design in the context of web development means creating web pages that are compatible with all devices that can connect to the internet and view webpages. A responsive web page will adjust the contents of the webpage to make itself look pleasing to the eyes and useable no matter what device you are using.


Let’s say for example, you are viewing a web article about the news before you head to work. You are reading it on your iPad while you are having your morning coffee. You have not been able to finish your read so you switch to your iPhone and continue while on your way to work. (Let’s assume you are using public transport to go to work. i.e. bus or subway.) Then, when you arrive to your desk. You finish up on the last few paragraphs before you boss arrives and you clock in your hours for the day.


The web article you were reading was done with responsive web design in mind. The web page adjusted itself as you switched from your iPad to your iPhone to your MacBook. Each of which has a different screen size and aspect ratio.
